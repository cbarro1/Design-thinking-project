The Crisis We Can't Ignore

Fast fashion's affordability and speed come at severe environmental and social costs. Gen Z and Millennials use fashion to express identity, yet this unintentionally fuels overconsumption.


Over 100 billion garments are produced annually, contributing to 10% of global carbon emissions, labor exploitation, and overproduction. Social media FOMO drives constant trend cycles. Sustainable options often feel too expensive or inaccessible.


The core challenge: redesign consumption so sustainable choices feel as exciting, validating, and expressive as fast fashion.

Our Net-Positive Innovation

A dating-app-inspired platform that matches users with sustainable brands based on their style and values.

Users build a style profile (aesthetic, brands they love, causes they care about). EcoMatch connects them to verified sustainable companies aligned with their identity. This solves the 'I never know what to wear' frustration and eliminates greenwashing confusion.

It makes sustainable discovery fun, effortless, and identity-driven.

1-Minute Pitch: "Imagine if your dating app cared about the planet as much as you do. What if it could match you with brands that share your values, your style, and your sense of self? That's EcoMatch. We're not just selling clothes—we're redesigning how people think about fashion. By connecting consumers with sustainable brands through a dating-app interface, we make ethical shopping feel as exciting, personal, and effortless as swiping. No more greenwashing. No more guesswork. Just brands that get you, and the planet."

Net Positive by Design

Reduces textile waste through outfit generation & mindful shopping. Supports ethical labor. Encourages slower, economical fashion. Builds community around conscious consumption. Strengthens resale, rental, and repair markets.
